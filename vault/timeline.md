# Timeline

## 1989

- Work on the standard diskless school computer classroom began.
- Existing commercial products such as NetWare and LANtastic were considered unsuitable because the client machines had no hard disks and the required hardware was unavailable or unaffordable.
- Development of a custom physical network, protocol, DOS driver, and user-storage model began.

## 1990

- Around the middle of the year, the first classroom was installed.
- Further installations followed as the system became a deployed product.

## 2026

- 2026-07-20: reconstruction of the project began from the original author's memories.
